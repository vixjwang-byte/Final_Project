Senior | Psychology & UX Design @ University of Michigan

Hi, I’m Vivian. I am particularly interested in how people make decisions, work together, and experience technology. My work sits at the intersection of behavioral science and organizational psychology, where I explore how environments, digital or social, shape human motivation, performance, and collaboration. I’m passionate about applying psychological insights to improve both user experiences and workplace systems, bridging people and business. I use research-driven methods to design intuitive, inclusive products and to inform organizational practices, linking human needs with strategic goals in ways that are practical, ethical, and grounded in real behavior.
